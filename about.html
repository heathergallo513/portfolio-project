The Story

Aside from being part of the entire Myspace generation who was fluent in marquee text and embedding strictly Sepia toned images through Photobucket, I'm a complete newcomer to coding in the professional sense. I've worked within a few different industries, but there was always a need to work creatively and problem-solve that hadn't been met. Despite feelings of imdimidation and imposter syndrome while hitting the initial "sign up," going through the courses led by Matt Delac and the amazing support from the tech profesionals on our Slack channels has affirmed by decision for a new career path. It's by far one of the hardest challenges I've had to push through, but the outcome has been worth it every time. I can't wait to see how my work continues to evolve as shown in just the first 3 projects!
